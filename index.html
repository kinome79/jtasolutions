A quick Bio...

I am always on the look out for my next challenging opportunately. Having been a hardware systems engineer for the last 15 years, I have a wide range of exposure to hardware and the software that runs on it. While it was a satisfying career, my passion for programming has me searching for other opportunities to put this natural talent to good use.

Having initially surpassed my highschool programming instructor, I was forced to pursue programming as independent study. At 20 I had already programmed Access databases for Honda Research and Developement before attending Ohio State University. Circumstances had me joining a hardware service company which began my server career. Since then I have had my hands in thousands of systems, have performed hardware replacements in 5 different countries, and trained many fellow engineers in various system types, including living in Japan for a year to train our remote team. Now having resigned from the hardware side and spent some time familiarizing myself with current technologies, I look to develope more innovative and helpful software solutions to assist people in their business and personal endevors.

Please review my projects and certifications, and request a resume from the contact section. I look forward to working with you!
